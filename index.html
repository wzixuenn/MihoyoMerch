About Us

Mihoyo Merchandise Store Mihoyo Merchandise Store is your ultimate destination for all things miHoYo and HoYoverse — a one-stop platform dedicated to fans who want to bring their favorite games to life through authentic, high-quality merchandise.

Our journey began in 2025, as a small passion project created by fans, for fans. What started as a humble idea quickly grew into a vibrant community hub where Hoyoverse enthusiasts from around the world can connect, collect, and celebrate the universes they love — from Genshin Impact and Honkai: Star Rail, to Zenless Zone Zero and Honkai Impact 3rd.

At Mihoyo Merchandise Store, we believe gaming is more than just a hobby — it’s an experience, a story, and a source of inspiration. That’s why our mission is to bring you beautifully crafted products that capture the spirit, artistry, and emotion behind every character and world. From stunning apparel and collectibles to limited-edition items and accessories, each piece is carefully selected to ensure quality, authenticity, and creativity. With community at our core, we’re constantly evolving to meet the expectations of passionate fans like you. Whether you’re a seasoned adventurer of Teyvat, a Trailblazer exploring the stars, or a newcomer diving into the chaos of New Eridu, we’re here to make sure you can proudly showcase your love for these incredible worlds.
